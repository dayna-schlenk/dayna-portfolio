About Me

I’m originally from Wisconsin, but currently live in Illinois. For several years, I was focused on becoming an elementary school teacher. During my senior year of college, I realized teaching wasn’t for me and changed my major to Family & Consumer Sciences. With that degree, I spent my first year out of college in the community services field. It was then when I realized something more was missing in my career.

I transitioned into recruitment, which is where I currently am full-time. I was first introduced to the world of tech a couple years ago by a family member. Once I realized that was a career option for me, I became dedicated to learning every technology and tool I possibly could. One of my goals for the future is to transition into web development full-time. I’ve always had a strong passion for creating and helping others, and I’m happy to have found websites and web apps as the medium for that.

Outside of my professional life, I like to spend time outdoors - hiking, biking and kayaking, mostly. I live with my boyfriend, Tim, and our dog, Picasso. Together, we like to watch true crime documentaries and cooking competition shows. Tim is also good about letting me constantly solicit user feedback from him.
